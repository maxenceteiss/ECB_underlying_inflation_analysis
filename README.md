# ECB Underlying Inflation Analysis

This repository contains supplementary materials associated with a research project on the emergence and evolution of underlying inflation in European Central Bank (ECB) communications, policy documents, and research publications.

## Contents

- `Appendix.html`: online appendix.
- `appendix_topic_list.html`: detailed topic descriptions and supplementary results.

## Project overview

The project relies on a large corpus of ECB documents and combines large-scale text processing with Structural Topic Modeling (STM) to study the role of underlying inflation in ECB communication and scientific production.

## Replication materials

The complete replication package, including corpus construction scripts, text-processing pipelines, and STM analyses, will be added to this repository in future updates.
